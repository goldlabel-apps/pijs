About Raspberry Pi

Raspberry Pis are very low cost, credit-card sized computers. It is a capable little device that enables people of all ages to explore computing, and to learn how to program in languages like Python. It can also give more experienced developers a chance to do some mad cool stuff they wouldn’t normally do. We’ve turned our Pi into a Node server, connected to the interweb through an everyday broadband connection.

The current iteration (which we are using) is the Pi4, or to giv it it’s full title, Raspberry Pi 4 Model B (4 GB) which you can buy from littlebird for $AU 100 (in Australia). There will no doubt be an easily Googleable supplier in your country.
